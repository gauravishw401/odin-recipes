Samosa

Description

The flavor and smell is the one that you think of when you think of Indian food: warm, spicy, fragrant. The pastry tasted good, but was quite crumbly. Modern samosa dough has baking powder in it, which wouldn't have been available at the time, but will make the samosas puff up and be nice and crispy.

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Wheat flour
  • sunflower oil
  • Spices

Steps

  1. Mix all-purpose flour, salt, and oil or ghee in a bowl.
  2. Rub the oil or ghee into the flour with your fingertips until it resembles breadcrumbs, according to RecipeTin Eats
  3. Gradually add water and knead until a firm, non sticky dough forms
  4. Cover the dough and let it rest for at least 30 minutes
